Our Children

Some of the children are internally Displaced Persons as a result of the Boko Haram attacks in the north-eastern Parts of Nigeria. Almost all the children have been traumatized due to the crisis, loss or neglect. Their stories are intense:... some stood by helplessly and watched their paents ruthlessly killed, while others fled to neighbouring lands and trekked long distances before getting any help. For most of them coming to House of Recab was the completion of a journey

Home

House of Recab strives to go beyond simply providing accomodation. We believe that when our children know that they are at home, they grow up to become more productive.

Education

Our approach to education is holistic. We believe that as a complete human, every aspect of a child's life needs to be developed ... in order for them to have a fulfilling life and and becoe relevant to our present and future societal problems

Trauma healing & counselling

We have ongoing trauma healing and counselling sessions offered to our children to help them heal from the past and focus on the exciting future that awaits them

Skills Acquisition

We believe that every child has an inherent skill. We try to uncover individual skills by exposing them to different areas that may be of interest to them.

Arts and Sports

Among many other benefits, sporting activities help eliminate mental exhaustion most especially for the children, including dance, music, drama, etc. Which help with trauma healing. ... With the merits of sports and arts in mind, we are working hard to expose the children to a variety of these activities

Why we need You

The task associated with helping these children is huge but not unsurmountable. We cannot do this alone, but with people whose hearts are touched by the stories and realities of Our Children in the House of Recab

We are a non-governmental charity established in 2013 to create an environment for orphans and vulnerable children where they can find courage and hope to journey towards the fulfillment of their destiny. We have over 200 children (most of whom are between 5 and 15) catered for by 39 passionate and dedicated staff.
